## 6. MODULE WRITING GUIDELINES

Based on analysis of existing modules:

### Format:
- **Screens**: 6-9 screens per module (avg: 7-8)
- **Words**: 30-60 words per screen
- **Total**: 200-500 words per module
- **Time**: 5-10 minutes per module (student completion)

### Structure:
1. **Introduction screen** - Why this matters, what you'll learn
2. **Content screens** - Core concepts with examples
3. **Closing screen** - Key takeaways or call to action

### Style:
- **Conversational and accessible**
- **Use stories and examples**
- **Explain jargon when used**
- **Include real-world applications**
- **Add citations for claims**

### Markdown Format:
```markdown
---
title: "Module: [Title]"
slug: "module-[slug]"
author: "SAUFEX Consortium"
date: "2026-01-23"
description: "[Brief description]"
learningPath: "[Learning Path Name]"
moduleNumber: [X]
estimatedTime: "8 minutes"
---

# Module: [Title]

[screen 1]

[30-60 words intro]

[screen 2]

[30-60 words content]

...

[screen N]

[30-60 words conclusion]
```
