## A preliminary list of ten choices
- Risk-oriented versus rights-oriented
- Content-oriented versus procedural and technical compliance-oriented
- Centralization-oriented versus decentralization-oriented
- Institution-oriented versus citizen-oriented
- Privacy-oriented versus service-oriented
- Autonomy-oriented versus identity-oriented
- Sender-oriented versus intermediator-oriented versus recipient-oriented
- Performance-oriented versus equity-oriented
- Accountability-oriented versus efficiency-oriented
- Preventive-oriented versus reactive-oriented
