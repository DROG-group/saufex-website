Lithuanian think tank Debunk.org analyzed nearly 20,000 disinformation cases from 2015–2026 as part of the EU-funded SAUFEX project, finding that Poland is the primary target of Russian disinformation in the European Union. The operation is continuous, multi-channel, and conducted in over 20 languages.

Key narratives attack Poland's history, portray it as a warmonger allied with Ukraine, and increasingly frame it as an aggressor provoking Russia — suggesting rhetorical groundwork for potential hostile actions.
